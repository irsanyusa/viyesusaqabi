Michelle Obama To Undergo Plastic Surgery After 50?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 5, 2024

Barack Obama's wife passed a strong message to women, via her People Magazine interview, focusing on keeping their health in good shape always, "Women should have the freedom to do whatever they need to do to feel good about themselves. Right now, i don’t imagine that i would go that route, but I've also learned to never say never."

The soon-to-be 50 years old beauty admitted to have never missed a health check up; including mammograms and Pap smears. She’s also had a colonoscope, according to RadarOnline. Mrs. Obama says, "I don’t obsess about what I eat, but I do make sure that I’m eating vegetables and fruits and as everyone knows, I do exercise."

Michelle turns 50 on Saturday and reports say the President is planning a huge feast for his wife at the White House but answering to whether she has reached her peak at 50, she jokingly said, "Being First Lady is pretty high up there. I’ve always felt that my life is ever-evolving and i don’t have the right to just sit on my talents or blessings".
