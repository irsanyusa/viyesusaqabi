Comedian arrested after fighting with teenager

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Katt Williams, 44, and Luke Walsh, 17, were both charged with disorderly conduct after they duelled at an apartment complex, Gainesville. This had been earlier revealed to the  The Atlanta Journal-Constitution by Police Sgt. Kevin Holbrook who also added that Walsh, the teenager, is being charged as an adult in the case.

Williams has been booked on an arrest order for contravening his conditional bond in addition to the disorderly conduct charge out of Gainesville, a police spokesperson said.

The troubled comedian, who has been asked to seek assistance, is being held by police without bond, and will appear before a judge today, March 31 2016.
