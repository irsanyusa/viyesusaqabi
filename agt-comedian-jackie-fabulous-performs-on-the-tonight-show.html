America’s Got Talent comedian Jackie Fabulous appeared on The Tonight Show Tuesday night to perform a stand-up routine. She joked about everything from moving out of her mother’s house to marrying a former athlete.

Jackie Fabulous Performs on The Tonight Show

Jackie Fabulous started her set by saying she was gearing up to visit relatives in Jamaica this summer. She went on to joke that, while she’s normally considered a 6, she’s a 49.6 in Jamaica. Fabulous added, “It’s not safe for me to walk around in Jamaica.”

She went on to talk about finally moving out of her mother’s house. Fabulous explained that her mother hates comedy, and she tells people that her daughter is “a joke.” She’ll also call Fabulous to tell her about TV shows she’s watching. Her Jamaican accent makes it difficult to understand what she’s talking about.

Fabulous also shared that she’s been married for six months now to an ex-athlete. She joked that women have to “find men when they’re at the end,” adding, “The more these guys’ bodies fall apart, the more they want to commit to you.”

Fabulous Competed on AGT Season 14

Fabulous was a contestant on Season 14 of America’s Got Talent, earning four “yes” votes from the judges in her audition. Her funny comedy performances ended up taking her all the way to the Semifinals, at which point she was eliminated.

However, the comedian returned earlier this year as a contestant on AGT: All-Stars. She joked about turning 50 and getting married. Unfortunately, she didn’t earn enough votes from the Superfans to move on to the Finals.

As Tonight Show host Jimmy Fallon shared before and after her recent performance, Fabulous will be performing this Saturday at the Historic Everett Theatre in Everett, Washington. The comedian also shared on Instagram that she’ll be performing on Sunday at the Tacoma Comedy Club in Tacoma, Washington.
