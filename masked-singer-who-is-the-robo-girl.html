The Masked Singer season 8 is cranking it up a notch and kicking into a new gear! That’s because they have a crazy new format full of twists, 22 contestants, and a lot of potential talent to come. Plus, with a shiny new slate of costumes, there’s plenty of wacky masks to see!

Now, we’ve had the Robot before, but we’ve never had a Robo Girl! And seeing how Lil Wayne fared as the Robot, let’s hope Robo Girl can make it farther than he did. Will there be a talented singer beneath the mask? Or will she be a victim of the double eliminations? We’ll go through everything we know about Robo Girl!

Clues on The Masked Singer Season 8

With the cutest metal pigtails, Robo Girl will take the stage with almost no clues pointing to her identity ahead of time! The Masked Singer has released one clue about the singer on Instagram. The mask-ray detects a wrench in her costume, officially starting the guessing game for Robo Girl!

The other clues we saw were a surfboard, jars of honey, and word that she’s worked with Missy Elliott, Pharrell Williams, and David Blaine. She mentioned that “with 86 cents to [her] name, [she] booked a job” that changed her life forever. She also talked about having “supernatural” confidence. Robo Girl revealed that she uses a stage name.

Popular Guesses for The Masked Singer Robo Girl

Robin thought it could be Jenna Dewan under the mask. Nicole predicted Becky G or Keke Palmer. And Ken Jeong went from Lucy Hale to Cara Delevigne to Selena Gomez. Without much to work with, fans online have already started their Internet sleuthing to try to figure out who Robo Girl is. Some popular guesses online are Sofia Carson, Camila Mendes, and Madonna.

Who is the Robo Girl on The Masked Singer?

After her first performance, the clues all lined up to point to one person. Based on that, we are predicting that Robo Girl is…

Kat Graham!

The actress, singer, and dancer was the Queen of her first episode! And here are the clues that prove it’s her.

Kat goes by a stage name when she releases music under the name Toro Gato. She appeared in the movie Honey 2, which explains the honey clue. She is from Los Angeles and has won two Teen Choice Awards, explaining the surfboard clue. Her work with Missy Elliott and Pharrell Williams was as a backup dancer, and she’s posted on Instagram at a David Blaine show. The “supernatural” reference could definitely point to her role as Bonnie Bennett on The Vampire Diaries.

Finally, the clue that really gave her away was saying at one point she had “86 cents to her name,” an exact quote that Kat Graham has used in interviews before. That’s just way too specific to be a coincidence!

Tune in to The Masked Singer every Wednesday, because Robo Girl could be unmasked any week now!
