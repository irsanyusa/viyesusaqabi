10celebrities who went makeup-free onthe red carpet and looked absolutely stunning

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 27, 2024

There is a new trend among celebrities to appear on the red carpet with little or no makeup. This is a really challenging experience as we’re all used to seeing our favorite celebrities with perfect makeup. You have to be really brave to go against the stereotypes of society and pose in front of the cameras au naturel.

We at Bright Side can’t stop admiring these women who are not afraid to show off their real beauty.

Alicia Keys

Alicia is a member of the ’no makeup’ movement and often appears at events and on the covers of magazines with no makeup. ’I hope to God it’s a revolution,’ Keys said, ’because I don’t want to cover up anymore.’

Jennifer Garner

The actress supported Alicia Keys and chose to go to the Telluride Film Festival makeup-free and dressed in sports jeans and a coat.

Amanda Seyfried

Amanda says that she isn’t a fan of bright makeup and that in everyday life she doesn’t wear makeup at all.

Tilda Swinton

We hardly ever see Tilda Swinton with bright makeup. She is convinced that it makes you look like someone else.

Gisele Bündchen

This supermodel usually wears so little makeup that it can barely be seen.

Jennifer Lawrence

Jennifer Lawrence appeared almost bare-faced at the premiere of The Great Gatsby. This gave rise to many discussions, however no one could deny that the actress looked quite stunning and natural.

Heidi Klum

The famous model doesn’t like to spend lots of time on makeup. ’I tend to not wear makeup on my off days. I am not complicated. I don’t have tons of creams or anything like that. I stay simple,’ says Heidi.

Natalia Vodianova

You rarely see this supermodel with makeup in everyday life. On the red carpet, Natalia looks naturally gorgeous with minimal makeup.

Naomi Watts

Naomi also enjoys appearing on the red carpet almost makeup-free.

Kate Winslet

’I want to embrace the real me, and I want you to embrace who you are, the way you are, and love yourself just the way you are,’ said Kate Winslet.
