The 6 Best Home Laser Hair Removal Devices

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Getting rid of unwanted hair can be done by waxing or epilating, but it’s not always very comfortable or effective. Thankfully, in 2008, the very first laser hair removal device was approved by the FDA and there are now many proven and safe products out there to choose from. Some aren’t exactly cheap (though you can still find some on a budget), so it’s important to know all the facts before making a purchase.

Here at Bright Side, we love innovation, as well as saving money, so if you’re sick of spending a fortune waxing at the salon, look no further.

1. Phillips Lumea Advanced IPL

Buy the Phillips Lumea Advanced IPL from Amazon here.

This hair removal device is highly effective, with 85% hair reduction in only 3 treatments. Unlike other brands where you need to use the device weekly, you only need to use this one once every 2 weeks for the first 4 weeks, and then once a month for a quick touch-up. It comes with 2 attachments, one for the body and one for the face. It also has an automatic sensor that measures your skin complexion and at the beginning of each session, and if it detects a skin tone that is too dark for this device, it will stop automatically.

PROS
If you’re looking for quick results without needing to use the device very often, you will like this device. It’s from a trusted brand and comes with a guarantee.

CONS
It’s not exactly on the cheap side, so if you’re on a budget, there are definitely better choices for you out there.
