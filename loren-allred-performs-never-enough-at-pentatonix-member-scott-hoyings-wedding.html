Loren Allred Performs 'Never Enough' At Pentatonix Member Scott Hoyings Wedding

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 22, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Isaiah Trickey/FilmMagic, Photo by Amy Sussman/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Loren Allred is among the people celebrating Pentatonix member Scott Hoying and his husband Mark Manio. The former Britain’s Got Talent stand out recently sang live at the couple’s wedding, serving as voice to the newlyweds’ first dance.

Loren Allred Serenades Scott Hoying, Mark Manio During Their Wedding

On July 15, Allred posted a short video on Instagram to congratulate Hoying and Manio with their recently held nuptials. According to her, she had the honor of “serenading my dear friends” for their first dance. Allred particularly sang “Never Enough” from the Disney film The Greatest Showman.  

“Cheers to the newlyweds @scotthoying and @markmanio. I love you both!” she wrote.

In the video, Allred revealed that “Never Enough” is a special song to the couple’s relationship. Recounting Hoying’s request, The Voice alum stated that she was “over the moon” when the Pentatonix member asked her for a favor.

Within the video, the singer was wearing a simple gold and black gown while belting the song out, complementing the whole vibe of the event. Hoying, who was clearly emotional during the first dance, took to the comment section to express his gratitude to the singer.

“Literally the most epic moment of our whole life! You are a force, a talent, an ANGEL and we love you so so much!!!! The honor was all ours,” Hoying wrote.

Manio also commented on Allred’s post, stating that they are “eternally grateful” for her. He went on and wrote that they will never forget their first dance for the rest of their lives.

She Recently Posted a BTS Recording of Disney’s “Reflection”

Just last week, Allred revealed a video snippet of her recording of Disney’s “Reflection.” In the clip, the singer can be seen powerfully belting the Mulan classic.

“Behind the scenes singing ‘Reflection’ for Disneyland! Make sure to check out the beautiful World of Color show at Disneyland California Adventures,” she wrote.

Earlier this year, Allred reflected on this milestone, treating the opportunity as a dream come true.

“Never thought my childhood dream of singing ‘Reflection’ at Disneyland would come true, but life surprises you in the most magical ways,” she wrote. “Grateful beyond words. Dreams really do come true.”
