How 15 of the Most Beautiful Female Celebrities Over 40 Rank According to Ordinary Women

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, August 26, 2024

The Glowday Beauty Clinic conducted a survey asking ordinary women which “over 40” celebrities they thought managed to preserve their beauty the best. It turned out that, of the women that made the top 15, there were some that don’t devote a lot of time to taking care of themselves.

15. Kate Beckinsale, 47

LISA O'CONNOR / AFP / East News

Kate is the actress you might remember from Pearl Harbor, Van Helsing, and the Underworld trilogy. 8% voted for her. She just uses inexpensive soap, sunscreen, and oils in her self-care routine.

14. Claudia Winkleman, 49

Phil Loftus / Capital Pictures / Capital Pictures / East News

The English TV host says that she doesn’t have any actual skincare routine and sometimes even goes to bed with her makeup on. The only thing she can recommend is to sleep more.

13. Helen Mirren, 75

Niviere David / ABACAPRESS.COM / Abaca / East News

Despite her age, the actress still stars in movies. She doesn’t think she is beautiful. She also doesn’t have any skin care advice, but if she was in a pharmacy with $20, she’d buy a lipstick, a moisturizer, and a lip liner.

12. Davina McCall, 53

Davina is an English actress and TV host. She has a strict daily schedule — she wakes up early in the morning, looks in the mirror often, takes vitamins and tea with collagen, and only eats healthy food. In her free time, she prefers baths with bath salt and a good book.

11. Kate Hudson, 41

face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News

It’s not surprising that this American actress is on the list. She’s very careful about her appearance and diet: she has a big arsenal of skin care products, has had beauty procedures, and uses face masks even while flying. She drinks a lot of water and meditates. Mental and physical health are her foundation of beauty.

10. Sarah Jessica Parker, 56

KELA / Broadimage / Broad Image / East News

The star of Sex and the City says that professionals are responsible for her gorgeous hair, and she doesn’t use any special beauty products. Any soap and shampoo, and a moisturizing cream every morning and evening.

9. Nigella Lawson, 61

Nigella is a British journalist, TV host, and cookbook author. She has shared a life hack: she uses silicone gloves for a bizarre skin-smoothing method where she uses them to exfoliate.

8. Reese Witherspoon, 45

face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News

The Oscar-winner and star of Big Little Lies looks younger than her age for a reason. She combines high-intensity dance cardio with body weight and resistance training. She eats a lot of greens, doesn’t skimp on sunscreen, always takes water with her, and spends a lot of time on herself, even though she has kids.

7. Kate Moss, 47

STEPHANE DE SAKUTIN / AFP / East News

Kate is a supermodel and she got 15% of the votes. She has an interesting trick in her arsenal: fill a sink with ice cubes, cold water, and slices of cucumber and hold your face in it for as long as you can. It instantly reduces puffiness and tightens up your skin.

6. Tess Daly, 51

WENN.com / agefotostock / East News

Tess is a journalist and TV host. She says that she carefully cleanses, tones, and moisturizes her skin every morning and evening, and spends a lot of time meditating, doing yoga, and swimming. In the morning, she uses a jump rope to quickly wake up.

5. Naomi Campbell, 50

Jonas Gustavsson / Sipa USA / East News

Another supermodel got onto the list with 20% of the votes. She uses a facial roller, moisturizing sprays and creams, does her makeup only before work, and always washes it off before sleep. She says that her skin has to be shiny and moisturized.

4. Victoria Beckham, 46

Fred Duval / MEGA / Mega Agency / East News

It’s hard to argue with the fact that the British singer and designer deserves such a high place on the list. Victoria has facial massages, does light therapy, uses natural products, is a fan of eye patches, and can’t imagine her life without skin moisturizers.

3. Kim Kardashian, 40

face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News

Kim has had a huge influence on makeup artists around the entire world, so her main secret is great makeup. She also adds a spoonful of sugar to shower gel to exfoliate her skin.

2. Jennifer Aniston, 52

Invision / Invision / East News

Everyone knows Jennifer not only because she’s a talented actress but also because she’s incredibly attractive. She has a lot of willpower. She washes her face in icy water, does infrared therapy, works out and does meditation, uses organic cosmetics, and drinks celery juice instead of coffee. She rarely uses beauty products and avoids air-drying her hair.

1. Jennifer Lopez, 51

face to face / FaceToFace / REPORTER / East News

The actress and singer got 38% of the votes and became the absolute leader, beating Aniston by 4%. At the same time, she doesn’t like talking about age or appearance because her philosophy is that beauty doesn’t depend on time. Her main tricks are sincerity, controlling her emotions, and... olive oil which her mom taught her to love.

Do you agree with this list? Who do you think deserves a higher (or a lower) position?
