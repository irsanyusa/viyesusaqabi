7+ Celebrity Couples Who Got Engaged Lightning-Fast

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Experts believe a waiting period of 3 to 6 months is needed in order for people to understand whether their romantic feelings are love or simply lust. However, there are couples who get engaged in less than a year after they started dating, including a few celebrities who were very quick to propose. Among those who said “yes” the fastest, there are some A-listers who are proving how strong their love has been from the get-go.

Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker — 9 months

Chris Pratt and Katherine Schwarzenegger — 7 months

Nikki Reed and Ian Somerhalder — 6 months

Sofía Vergara and Joe Manganiello — 6 months

AFP/EAST NEWS

Carey Mulligan and Marcus Mumford — 5 months

Invision/Invision/East News

Nick Jonas and Priyanka Chopra Jonas — 2 months

Justin Bieber and Hailey Bieber — 1 month

Bonus: George Clooney and Amal Clooney — 7 months

Collin Xavier/Image Press Agency ABACA/Abaca/East News

George Clooney didn’t take a long time before asking Amal Alamuddin to marry him. Despite dating for only seven months, he also created a really romantic scenario to propose to Amal.

Hahn Lionel/ABACA USA/EAST NEWS, Collin Xavier/Image Press Agency ABACA/Abaca/East News

But once Amal saw the ring, she looked confused, and she didn’t even say yes for about 25 minutes.

“We now know, because there was a playlist, it was like 25 minutes! And I finally said, ’I hope the answer’s yes, but I need an answer, because I’m 52, and I could throw out my hip pretty soon.’ And then she said yes. So it worked out pretty well,” Clooney revealed.

On the other hand, not everyone is as quick to get engaged or even to tie the knot. In the world of entertainment, there are also plenty of power couples who waited a longtime before putting a ring on it. One couple in particular waited 28 years before getting married, and their response to that long period was iconic.
