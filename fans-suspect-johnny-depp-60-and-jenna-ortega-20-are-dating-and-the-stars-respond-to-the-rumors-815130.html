Fans Suspect Johnny Depp, 60, and Jenna Ortega, 20, Are Dating and the Stars Respond to the Rumors

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

The pair was recently “spotted together” and the rumors started spreading like wildfire. Some fans are assured that 20-year-old Jenna Ortega is dating 60-year-old Johnny Depp. The “news” spread so fast that even the actors themselves couldn’t help but reacted to it.

They were “spotted together”.

It all began with a rumor that started spreading when a gossip account mistakenly claimed that Depp and Ortega had been seen together. The “news” went viral on the internet, and before you knew it, people were speculating like never before. Some were convinced they were dating, while others had theories that Depp might be working with Jenna in Beetlejuice 2.

Jenna addressed the rumors on Instagram.

CJ Rivera/Everett Collection/East News

The actress didn’t waste a second to clear things up about her supposed relationship with the Pirates of the Caribbean actor. She posted on her Instagram story, “This is so ridiculous I can’t even laugh. I have never met or worked with Johnny Depp in my life. Please stop spreading lies and leave us alone.”

Johnny also stepped in.

Julie Edwards / Avalon/Photoshot/East News

Depp’s spokesperson was quick to jump in and clarify everything. Their statement left no room for doubt, “Mr. Depp has no personal or professional relationship with Ms. Ortega whatsoever. He has never met her or spoken to her. He is not involved in any project with her, nor does he intend to be. He is appalled by these baseless and malicious rumors that are intended to harm his reputation and career.”

They both worked with Tim Burton.

Jenna and Johnny both collaborated with Tim Burton, which undoubtedly fuelled some of the rumors. For years, Depp was considered Burton’s preferred leading man. On the other hand, Ortega is a relative newcomer to Burton’s world, having recently starred as Wednesday Addams in Netflix’s series Wednesday. Additionally, Ortega will join the cast of the long-anticipated Beetlejuice 2.
