Singer celebrates curvy African women in 'Booty Bounce' video

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 7, 2024

Victoria Kimani, who has previously released chart-topping singles like ‘Show’, ‘Two Of Dem’, ‘Prokoto’ featuring Diamond Platnumz and Ommy Dimpoz, ‘Oya’ featuring M.I and a few others, is rapidly making her way to the top of the pick and set to break barriers with the release of ‘Booty Bounce’ that has already been termed a ‘game changer’ by various music pundits in and outside Africa.

'Booty Bounce’ is released as one of the singles offVictoria Kimani's highly anticipated debut album which is in the works. And against popular expectations of the new video, Victoria Kimani keeps it simple and decent, portraying the true beauty of African women.

Victoria Kimani's ‘Booty Bounce’ is definitely a tune to herald the holiday seasons with and is sure to be on everyone's lips in no time.
