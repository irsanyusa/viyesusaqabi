Singer tattoos Lil Wayne's name on her arm

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 3, 2024

The reality TV star claims its her first ever tattoo with someone's name on her body. Her tattoo reads ''Love hard T n T'' which means Tina n Tunechi - the couple's nicknames.

The singer however has no tattoo bearing her ex husband, The Dream's name, or that of her ex-fiance, Jas Prince or even that of her daughter or any member of her family... just Wayne.

She again posted a photo of a new gift from the rapper [probablt for Mother's Day] today, May 12: a bracelet bearing the same TnT signature. Milian captioned: ''Perfect. Thank you Bae. T n T #LoveOnFleek''
