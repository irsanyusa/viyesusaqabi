15 Celebrity Couples Who Kill the Stigma That Tall Women Cant Date Short Guys

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

One study suggests that women are way more selective about their partner’s height than men. For ladies, the bigger the height difference the better, but with the condition that the man is taller. And yet, our height has nothing to do with the kind of people we are, so why should it affect our decisions when it comes to love? Take some famous couples for example. There are many examples of women being taller than their boyfriends in Hollywood and they look as happy as can be.

We at Bright Side are on a mission to break yet another stereotype and we have a selection of celebrity women that don’t care about their partner’s height to help with that.

1. Joe Jonas (5’7’’) and Sophie Turner (5’9’’)

2. Daniel Radcliffe (5’5’’) and Erin Darke (5’7’’)

3. Kevin Hart (5’4’’) and Eniko Hart (5’7’’)

4. James Conran (5’8’’) and Coco Rocha (5’10’’)

5. Tom Cruise (5’8’’) and Katie Holmes (5’9’’)

6. Michael J. Fox (5’4’’) and Tracy Pollan (5’6’’)

7. Keith Urban (5’10’’) and Nicole Kidman (5’11’’)

8. Benji Madden (5’5’’) and Cameron Diaz (5’9’’)

9. Danny DeVito (4’8’’) and Rhea Perlman (4’9’’)

10. Pharrell Williams (5’9’’) and Helen Lasichanh (5’11’’)

11. Jamie Mazur (5’8’’) and Alessandra Ambrosio (5’9’’)

12. Mark Walhberg (5’8’’) and Rhea Durham (5’9’’)

13. Sean Penn (5’8’’) and Charlize Theron (5’9’’)

14. Seth Green (5’4’’) and Clare Grant (5’7’’)

15. Ethan Hawke (5’10’’) and Uma Thurman (5’11’’)

Bonus: Peter Dinklage (4’4’’) and Erica Schmidt (5’6’’)

Do you think height difference matters in a relationship?
