Mothers Velcro Baby Trick on a Plane Seat Causes a Stir (Video)

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Lisa Flom, known for her cool parenting hacks, sparked mixed reactions with a TikTok video. The video showed her baby seated on a plane using binding tapes on the seat and toddler’s clothing. Many were curious about how she managed to pull off this unconventional hack. Let’s have a look.

Lisa hails from Eden Prairie in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA.

In a viral video that has gathered nearly 20 million views, Lisa Flom, a mother of two, can be seen placing her daughter on an airplane seat. Lisa used fastening strips to secure her baby, sparking controversy among viewers.

As the baby appeared to try to wriggle free and lean forward, some interpreted it as an attempt to escape from her seat. Lisa captioned the video with “The best airplane travel hack,” which garnered mixed reactions from viewers. One TikTok user raised concerns about the residue left by the tape for the next passenger.

Lisa is known for her unconventional parenting hacks.

Here are some comments from viewers:

  • “My child would start using their voice to express frustration within five seconds.”
  • “Putting the dog in the cargo area works too.”
  • “Why not just bring her car seat?”
  • “This seems like a good idea until the kid gets annoyed.”
  • “Does this work for 36-year-old kids?” (a joke)
  • “As a mother of 12, I find it safer and cheaper to put them in the cargo hold in one cage! Good luck, momma!”

One reader commented, “I’m just mad because I didn’t think of this.”

This way, Lisa is trying to make motherhood a bit easier.

Lisa, a mother of two daughters aged three and one, mentioned to Newsweek in January that the challenges of motherhood can sometimes feel overwhelming. With 809,000 followers on her TikTok page, she focuses on sharing mom hacks and tips. Lisa stated that her social channel is centered around conversations with other moms about making motherhood a bit easier through tips and tricks.

Some of Lisa’s popular tips have included using frozen wet nappies as oversized band-aids for bumps and encouraging potty training by letting kids color in the toilet with felt pens. She explained that as a parent, there are moments when she needs a few minutes to accomplish tasks with her energetic one-year-old.

Lisa believes that parenting can be enjoyable and filled with fun moments, and her content aims to provide ideas and hacks that not only ease the process but also bring laughter.

Lisa Flom’s innovative approach to parenting, as showcased in her viral TikTok videos, is both inspiring and refreshing. Her ability to think outside the box and come up with creative solutions to everyday parenting challenges has garnered admiration from parents worldwide. By sharing her unique hacks and tips, she has created a supportive online community where parents can exchange ideas and learn from each other’s experiences.

Through her videos, Lisa has not only provided practical advice but also infused a sense of fun and joy into the parenting journey. Her positive attitude and enthusiasm for finding solutions have resonated with many, making her a relatable and influential figure in the parenting sphere. The diverse range of topics she covers, from potty training tricks to innovative travel hacks, reflects her deep understanding of the daily struggles faced by parents.

Moreover, Lisa’s willingness to experiment and try new approaches has encouraged others to think creatively and embrace challenges with optimism.

Her success on TikTok, with a significant following of over 800,000 people, speaks volumes about the impact she has had on the parenting community. In a world where parenting advice can often feel overwhelming, Lisa’s down-to-earth approach and genuine desire to help others stand out as a beacon of positivity.

In conclusion, Lisa Flom’s contributions to the parenting community go beyond just tips and hacks; they embody a spirit of camaraderie, support, and joy. Her journey reminds us that parenting is not just about overcoming challenges, but also about finding delight in the everyday moments shared with our children.
