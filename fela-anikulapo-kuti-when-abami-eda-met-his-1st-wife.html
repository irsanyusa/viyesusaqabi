When 'Abami Eda' Met His 1st Wife

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 3, 2024

September 20, 2014 5:31 PM

Son of Afrobeat maestro, Femi Kuti shares a historic picture of when his dad, the late Fela met his mum Remi Kuti.

The Afrobeat legend met his first wife Remilekun (Taylor) Kuti while he was in London where he schooled at Trinity College of Music. The pair were married in 1960 and had three children Femi, Yeni and Sola.
